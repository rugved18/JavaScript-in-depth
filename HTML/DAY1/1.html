Membership Info

Join our library community today! Membership is free and open to all residents of Greenfield. Enjoy access to our extensive book collection, digital resources, and exclusive member events.

Types of Membership:

      Student (Age 10–18)
      Adult (Age 19–64)
      Senior (Age 65+)

Benefits:

  • Borrow up to 10 books at a time
  • Access to e-books and audiobooks
  • Invitations to exclusive events and workshops
  • Discounts on library merchandise

Required Documents:

  • Valid ID Proof (Aadhaar, School ID, etc.)
  • Proof of Residence (Utility Bill, Rental Agreement, etc.)
  • Passport-sized Photograph
